// The Story

FROM ONE ROOM TO EVERY CITY

It started with frustration. We were tired of paying premium prices for brands that didn't understand the Indian street.

In 2025, we set up in Chennai with a clear mission: make streetwear that speaks the language of Indian cities.

Urban Grain ships pan-India. Heavy fabrics, real stories, real streets.
